Group G18 Project Log

Week 5 — Initial Model Evaluation

Status: Completed | Dates: 25/05/2026 – 31/05/2026

Goals for the Week

  • Execute the first training runs of the baseline pipeline on the target datasets.
  • Write evaluation scripts and establish baseline performance metrics.

Tasks Completed

  • Ran initial evaluations of the baseline pipeline on the DSIFN-CD and WHU-CD datasets to verify the training script's stability over multiple epochs.
  • Developed the evaluation scripts to calculate standard region metrics including Precision, Recall, F1-score, and Intersection over Union (IoU).
  • Implemented custom evaluation functions to calculate boundary-specific metrics, specifically Boundary F1 (BF1) and Boundary IoU (BIoU) using a 3-pixel tolerance band.
  • Established our baseline benchmark scores to compare against future custom architectural improvements.

Technical Challenges

  • Ensuring the custom boundary evaluation scripts accurately extracted and matched binary edge pixels between the predicted masks and ground truth within the strict 3-pixel tolerance limit.

Next Steps

  • Proceed to Week 6: Data analysis and behavior assessment of these baseline models to pinpoint exact failure modes near object boundaries.
