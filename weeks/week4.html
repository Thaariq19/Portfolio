Group G18 Project Log

Week 4 — Implementation of the Experimental Pipeline

Status: Completed | Dates: 18/05/2026 – 24/05/2026

Goals for the Week

  • Construct the foundational deep learning pipeline in PyTorch.
  • Configure data ingestion for the high-resolution bi-temporal datasets.

Tasks Completed

  • Configured the PyTorch data loading and preprocessing pipelines for the DSIFN-CD and WHU-CD datasets, implementing 256x256 cropping and random horizontal/vertical flips.
  • Set up the foundational training loop utilizing the AdamW optimizer, a cosine learning rate schedule, and Automatic Mixed Precision (AMP).
  • Implemented standard baseline loss functions (BCE and Dice) to verify gradient flow through the experimental pipeline.
  • Integrated a standard MambaVision-S backbone without custom fusion modules to serve as an initial placeholder and test the data flow.

Technical Challenges

  • Optimizing the PyTorch DataLoader multiprocessing and batch sizes to prevent out-of-memory crashes on the local Acer Aspire 5 hardware before moving to heavier training clusters.

Next Steps

  • Proceed to Week 5: Execute initial training runs on the baseline setup and establish target evaluation metrics.

References

  • A. Hatamizadeh and J. Kautz, "Mambavision: A hybrid mamba-transformer vision backbone," in Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2025.
