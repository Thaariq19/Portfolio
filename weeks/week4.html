Group G18 Project Log

Week 4 — Experimental Pipeline Implementation & Dataset Setup

Status: Completed | Dates: 18/05/2026 – 24/05/2026

Goals for the Week

  • Implement the core MambaRefine-CD architecture, including the MambaVision-S encoder, D-RBI, and CRAM-lite, in code.
  • Configure the data loading pipelines and the training environment for the selected datasets.
  • Deliverable: Finalize the Literature Review and Initial Setup documentation.

Tasks Completed

  • Coded the shared MambaVision-S encoder and the CRAM-lite residual spatial modulation block.
  • Configured the data loading and preprocessing pipelines for the DSIFN-CD and WHU-CD datasets, integrating 256×256 cropping and random horizontal/vertical flips for data augmentation.
  • Implemented the compound training objective, combining BCE, Dice, coarse region loss, and a Sobel-edge l1 boundary loss (LBCE + LDice + 0.4Lcoarse + 0.1Lboundary).
  • Set up the training loop using the AdamW optimizer, a cosine learning rate schedule, and Automatic Mixed Precision (AMP) for memory efficiency.
  • Compiled and submitted the Week 4 deliverable: Summary of background research and initial framework.

Technical Challenges

  • Balancing the auxiliary loss weights for the coarse and boundary losses so they assist the network without overpowering the main BCE and Dice objectives during the initial training phases.

Next Steps

  • Proceed to Week 5: Initial model training, behavior assessment of baseline models, and debugging the training loop.
