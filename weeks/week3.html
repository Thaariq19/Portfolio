Group G18 Project Log

Week 3 — Benchmarking Framework & Architecture Design

Status: Completed | Dates: 11/05/2026 – 17/05/2026

Goals for the Week

  • Design a framework to separate temporal evidence into distinct region and boundary streams.
  • Draft the decoding architecture for the proposed MambaRefine-CD system.

Tasks Completed

  • Designed the Differential Region-Boundary Interaction (D-RBI) module. This module applies two learned gating mechanisms: a region gate for stable changed-area responses and a Sobel-conditioned boundary gate for contour-sensitive evidence.
  • Drafted the Adaptive Receptive-Field (ARF) FPN to decode region features.
  • Conceptualized a bounded residual refinement head that uses the finest boundary stream to apply controlled corrections to coarse logits, reducing the risk of overwriting the global prediction.

Technical Challenges

  • Formulating the Sobel-conditioned gate so that the edge cue is tied directly to temporal change evidence rather than ordinary image texture.

Next Steps

  • Proceed to Week 4: Begin coding the shared MambaVision encoder and the D-RBI module in PyTorch.
